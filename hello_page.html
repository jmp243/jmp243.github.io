Project: Part III - Usability Evaluation

Jung Mee Park, PhD is a data analyst at the University of Arizona's University Information Technology Services (UITS). Dr. Park holds a BA in history and sociology from the University of Pennsylvania, MA in Quantitative Methods in Social Sciences from Columbia University, and PhD in Sociology from Cornell University. Her publications have appeared in the American Journal of Sociology, International Area Studies Review, and the Journal of Education in Library and Information Sciences. She is a visiting scholar at the University of Southern California (USC), where she previously worked as a postdoctoral fellow. She enjoys hiking and playing music with her husband and son on the weekends.
